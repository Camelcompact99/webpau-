I view photography as a way of survival.

The world behind the world. With my photography I keep my faith alive, I take the world and make it mine. I try to order this ungovernable truth in a visceral and intuitive way, where the individuals and the spaces I portray appear unwavering and pure, always capturing the decisive moment of their presence, preventing them from lying to me.
