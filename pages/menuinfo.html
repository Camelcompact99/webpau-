info

I view photography as a way of survival.

As I organise this a priori ungovernable truth, in the midst of trying to govern it, I begin to discover the world that is hidden behind the world: my own world. With my photography I keep my Faith alive, I take the world and make it mine. Then, I show it to the rest. I order this ungovernable truth in a visceral and intuitive way, where the individuals and the spaces I portray appear unwavering and pure, always capturing the decisive moment of their presence, preventing them from lying to me, adding a mysterious and subtle aura to the images. This need to show a reality that only some capture in their dreams is the main reason for the creation of my pieces...
